Start of Design Career 2011

In 2012 I got a new job, it was a company called "Yard Design". I have worked there for almost three years and from a junior designer I have become a real specialist with a deep knowledge of design and a lot more.

I'm really proud that in "Yard Design" I've had a really great skilled art director that was teaching me.
