Access to millions of new, refurbished and used car parts


Norways largest disassembly company.
The company was established in 1992, and holds today a parts warehouse of approximately 5,500 square meters on a 23-acre plot just outside Brumunddal center. The warehouse counts over 50,000 indexed and searchable car parts, which makes GBD one of Norway’s largest suppliers of disassembled car parts. In addition, Grønvolds Bildemontering AS is the country’s largest recipient of insurance-condemned damaged cars.
