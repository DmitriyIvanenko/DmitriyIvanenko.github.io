Mobile Messenger


SENDER - is a new generation messenger that lets user talk to his friends easier and faster. With SENDER user interact with companies, receive invoices and pay for the goods and services directly in chat.

SENDER lets user:
easily talk to friends: send text, voice, video messages, smileys, funny stickers, create group chats and play games;
manage your finance through smartphone: fill your account or your friends, transfer money with the phone number or bank card;
cooperate with companies: order and pay for goods and services directly in chat, make payments both in online and ground shops;
work with the bank accounts: receive cash in ATMs, pay for the public utilities, and even issue an invoice to the debtor.
